Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I play the game?
A: This is an interactive story game. You progress by making choices for Henry. Tap on the option you want him to take and see what happens! Some choices lead to success, while others lead to famously funny failures.
Q: How many endings are there?
A: The game boasts 32 unique endings! Your choices across all chapters determine which ending you get. Exploring different paths is the key to discovering them all:cite[2]:cite[4].
Q: Is my progress saved?
A: Yes, your game progress and unlocked endings are saved automatically on your device.
Q: I purchased the game on another device. How do I restore it?
A: This game is a one-time purchase. To restore it on a new device, ensure you are logged into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. Then, simply go to the App Store page for the game and tap the download/purchase button again. You should see an option to "Restore Purchase" without being charged again. Note: The game does not use a separate login account system.
Q: Are there in-app purchases or ads?
A: No, This is a premium game with no in-app purchases and no advertisements.
Q: The game is crashing or not running properly. What should I do?
A: Please try the following steps:
1. Force close and restart the app.
2. Restart your iPhone or iPad.
3. Check for updates: Ensure your device is running the latest version of iOS/iPadOS. You can also check the App Store for any game updates.
4. Reinstall the game: As a last resort, you can delete and reinstall the app. Please note: Your save data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app will erase all your progress and unlocked endings.
